# PRD: Vibe & Sip
## Overview
Vibe & Sip helps casual home bartenders choose the perfect cocktail for the moment by selecting a vibe, not scrolling endless recipes. The MVP delivers a short, curated list of cocktails (3 per vibe) with simple, easy-to-follow recipes.

## Goals
Reduce decision fatigue for casual hosts.
Deliver fast, confident cocktail choices based on mood and effort.
Provide a lightweight, delightful experience with minimal steps.

## Non-Goals (Explicitly Out of Scope for MVP)
User accounts or login
Inventory tracking
AI-based cocktail generation
Social features
Affiliate links
Personas
Primary: The Casual Host (25–45)

Drinks socially at home; not a hobbyist bartender
Hosts friends, date nights, or chill evenings
Wants a thoughtful drink with low effort
Pain points: decision fatigue, fear of wrong choice, too many options online
Secondary (Post-MVP)

Couples planning at-home date nights
Content creators seeking inspiration
Occasional drinkers wanting “do it right”

## MVP Feature Requirements
1) Vibe Selection Screen (Required)
Vibes: Chill Night, Date Night, Party, Cozy, Fancy-but-Easy
Single selection at a time
2) Occasion Filter (Optional Toggle)
Options: Solo, Couple, Hosting Friends
Filter suggests but does not block recommendations
3) Difficulty Slider
Values: Lazy → Moderate → Impress
4) Curated Output (Core)
Exactly 3 cocktail recommendations per vibe
No endless lists or infinite scroll
5) Simple Recipe View
Ingredients list
Clear ratios
3–5 bullet steps max
Nice-to-Have (Only if Time Allows)
Save favorites
“Surprise me” button
Alcohol level tag (light / medium / strong)

## Revenue Model
Free Tier

Limited vibes
Limited daily recommendations
Premium Tier

Full vibe library
Seasonal collections
Unlimited saves
Offline access

## Success Metrics (MVP)
Time-to-decision: < 60 seconds from open to selection
Completion rate: % of sessions that open a recipe view
Repeat usage: weekly returning users

## User Flow (MVP)
Open app
Choose vibe
(Optional) Set occasion + difficulty
View 3 curated cocktails
Open a recipe

## Content & Data Requirements
Pre-curated cocktail catalog with tags:
Vibe
Occasion
Difficulty
Alcohol level
3 cocktails per vibe for MVP launch

## Risks & Mitigations
Risk: Users want more options
Mitigation: Keep strict 3-cocktail limit but add “Surprise me” later.
Risk: Confusion about difficulty
Mitigation: Use short labels (“Lazy / Moderate / Impress”) with brief hints.

## Later Expansion (Not for MVP)
Sponsored collections
Brand partnerships
Creator-curated packs
